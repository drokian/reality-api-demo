# Glossary

## Computational Reality

The idea that physical reality may operate through information-processing rules.

## Observation

A structured record of a concrete phenomenon, linked to confidence and source.

## Hypothesis

A testable claim about the computational structure of reality.

## Experiment

A planned procedure to validate or falsify a hypothesis.

## Analysis

A synthesis layer that connects observations, references, and hypotheses.

## Reference

An academic or technical source used to ground claims.

## Snapshot

The exported static JSON dataset used by the demo.

## Read-only Demo

Public interface where data can be viewed but not modified.

## Falsifiability

A scientific quality where a claim can, in principle, be proven wrong.
