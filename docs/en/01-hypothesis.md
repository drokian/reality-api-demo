# 01 - Core Hypothesis

## Statement

Reality may be a computed physical system.
This means reality is fully real, but its evolution follows computable rules.

## Working Implications

- Constants can be interpreted as systemic constraints.
- Observation may act as a state-selection trigger.
- Information density and processing behavior may be related.

## Evidence Strategy

1. Record structured observations.
2. Map each claim to references.
3. Build falsifiable hypotheses.
4. Test predictions through experiments and analysis.

## Confidence Discipline

Each claim should be tagged by confidence level and updated when new evidence appears.

## Demo Role

The demo presents this hypothesis as a research program, not a final conclusion.
